Our Story

A Legacy of Victorian Elegance

Built in 1894 for Frank P. and Anna Shepard, The New Victorian Mansion Bed & Breakfast stands proudly at 325 Dayton Avenue in Saint Paul's historic Cathedral Hill neighborhood. This stunning Queen Anne Victorian features Renaissance and Gothic design elements, showcasing intricately carved woodwork, grand fireplaces, and spacious guest areas.

A contributing structure within the Historic Hill District (added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1976), our mansion served as a Showcase Home for the American Society of Interior Designers in 2013. Experience the perfect blend of historic charm with contemporary comforts.
