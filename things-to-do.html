Discover Saint Paul

Explore the best of Cathedral Hill and beyond - dining, culture, outdoor adventures, and hidden gems just steps from our door.

Must See

Summit Avenue

Just steps from our door, Summit Avenue is the longest stretch of Victorian homes in America. Take a leisurely stroll past the historic James J. Hill House, the Governor's Residence, and dozens of stunning mansions. The tree-lined boulevard is perfect for morning walks or evening jaunts to nearby Grand Avenue.
