Your Comfort

Amenities & Services

Experience Victorian elegance with modern luxury touches

At The New Victorian Mansion, we believe your stay should be nothing short of extraordinary. From the moment you arrive until the moment you depart, every detail has been thoughtfully considered to ensure your comfort, relaxation, and enjoyment. Our blend of historic charm and modern conveniences creates an experience that honors the past while embracing today's expectations for luxury.

Chef's Choice Gourmet Breakfast

Begin each morning with a delicious gourmet breakfast, freshly prepared and served at 9:00 AM in our elegant dining room. Our rotating menu showcases locally sourced and organic ingredients whenever possible, ensuring every meal is a culinary delight.

  • Fresh seasonal fruits and house-made pastries
  • Signature entrées featuring local ingredients
  • Fresh-brewed coffee, premium teas, and juices
  • Dietary accommodations with advance notice
  • Breakfast-in-bed available for purchase

Modern Entertainment & Connectivity

While we cherish our Victorian heritage, we understand the importance of staying connected. Every suite is equipped with the latest technology to keep you entertained and productive during your stay.

  • Complimentary high-speed WiFi throughout
  • Smart TVs in every suite
  • Netflix, Apple TV, Amazon Prime, HBO, Hulu
  • Live TV channels available
  • Individual climate control in each suite

Luxurious Comfort Features

Every detail of your stay has been considered for maximum comfort. From the linens on your bed to the floors beneath your feet, we've invested in quality touches that make all the difference.

  • Premium bedding with luxury linens
  • Heated tile bathroom floors (select suites)
  • Soaking tubs and walk-in showers
  • Plush robes and premium toiletries
  • Automated lighting systems

Our Commitment

Eco-Friendly Practices

Sustainable Hospitality

We believe luxury and environmental responsibility go hand in hand. The New Victorian Mansion is committed to sustainable practices that protect our community and planet while providing you with a safe, healthy environment.

  • Steam and ozone-infused water sanitation systems
  • Non-toxic, eco-friendly cleaning products
  • Locally sourced breakfast ingredients
  • Partnerships with sustainable vendors
  • Energy-efficient lighting and climate systems
  • Recycling and composting programs
