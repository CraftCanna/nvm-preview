Royal Suite

Ultimate Luxury in Victorian Splendor

Premium Second Floor Suite

Indulgence Redefined

The Royal Suite represents the pinnacle of luxury at The New Victorian Mansion. Recently renovated with meticulous attention to detail, this expansive suite seamlessly blends Victorian grandeur with contemporary indulgence, creating an unforgettable retreat for discerning guests.

Center stage is the sumptuous King bed, dressed in premium linens and positioned to take advantage of the suite's generous proportions. Adjacent to the bedroom, a private sitting room provides the perfect space to relax, read, or enjoy morning coffee—your own Victorian parlor.

But the true crown jewel awaits in the bathroom: a large jetted soaking tub invites hours of relaxation, while the spacious walk-in shower features an impressive four showerheads for a spa-like experience. The decorative fireplace adds romantic ambiance, and heated bathroom floors ensure comfort in every step.

The Royal Experience

Live Like Royalty

From the moment you enter the Royal Suite, you'll understand why it's our most requested accommodation. The generous proportions, thoughtful design, and premium amenities create an atmosphere of refined luxury that envelops you in comfort.

Begin your day with a private moment in your sitting room, sunlight streaming through Victorian windows, before descending for a gourmet breakfast prepared by our chef. Explore the charming streets of Cathedral Hill, knowing that supreme relaxation awaits your return.

As evening falls, draw a bath in your jetted tub, light the decorative candles, and let the cares of the world melt away. This is the Royal Suite experience—a perfect blend of historic elegance and modern indulgence.

Perfect for Your Special Day

The Ultimate Bridal Suite

Planning an intimate wedding at The New Victorian Mansion? The Royal Suite serves as the perfect bridal suite, offering ample space for getting ready with your wedding party, a spa bathroom for pre-ceremony pampering, and a romantic retreat for your wedding night.

The separate sitting room provides space for hair and makeup, while the bedroom offers a peaceful sanctuary. After saying "I do," return to your private suite for champagne in the jetted tub and memories to last a lifetime.
