The Penthouse

Your Private Victorian Retreat Above the City

Third Floor Private Residence

A Home Away From Home

The Penthouse occupies the entire third floor of The New Victorian Mansion—once the private quarters for the estate's household staff in 1894, now transformed into a charming, cozy retreat perfect for families, groups of friends, or anyone seeking more space and privacy during their Saint Paul stay.

Unlike our romantic suites below, The Penthouse offers a self-contained living experience. Spread out across four bedrooms, relax in the bright living room with its decorative fireplace, prepare meals in the full kitchen, and enjoy the convenience of private laundry facilities. It's the best of both worlds: the character of a historic Victorian mansion with the comfort and privacy of your own apartment.

But the true magic happens when you look out the windows. From this elevated vantage point, you'll enjoy spectacular rooftop views of the magnificent St. Paul Cathedral—a sight that never gets old, whether bathed in morning light or illuminated against the night sky.

Four Distinct Spaces

The Penthouse Bedrooms

Each bedroom is named for the historic Saint Paul avenues visible from our hilltop location.

Dayton Room

Named for the avenue we call home, this cozy bedroom offers a comfortable bed and classic Victorian charm with views of the neighborhood.

Grand Room

Aptly named, this bedroom features generous proportions and elegant finishes, perfect for guests who appreciate extra space.

Summit Room

Inspired by the famous Summit Avenue, this room captures the elegance of Saint Paul's grandest thoroughfare.

Marshall Room

A bright and cheerful space named for the charming Marshall Avenue, one of the neighborhood's most beloved streets.

Space to Gather

Living Room & Common Areas

The heart of The Penthouse is its bright, welcoming living room. Natural light floods through the windows, offering those famous rooftop cathedral views that make this space so special. A decorative fireplace anchors the room, adding Victorian ambiance to your gatherings.

Settle into comfortable seating for movie nights with the full streaming lineup—Netflix, Apple TV, Amazon Prime, HBO Max, Hulu, and live TV. Or gather around the dining area for family meals prepared in your private kitchen.

Whether you're planning the day's adventures, playing board games with the family, or simply enjoying each other's company, the living room provides the perfect backdrop for making memories.
