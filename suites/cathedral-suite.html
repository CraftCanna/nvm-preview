Cathedral Suite

Where Historic Charm Meets Heavenly Views

Second Floor Turret Room

A Room with a View

Step into our beloved Cathedral Suite and discover why guests return year after year to this enchanting turret room. Perched on the second floor of our 1894 Queen Anne Victorian, this romantic retreat offers unparalleled views of the magnificent St. Paul Cathedral through its curved turret windows.

The suite masterfully blends bold historic character with tranquil warmth. Rich jewel tones complement the original architectural details, while modern amenities ensure your comfort. Sink into the luxurious Queen bed after a day exploring Cathedral Hill, or indulge in a relaxing soak in your private bubble massage tub.

The heated bathroom floors provide a welcoming touch on Minnesota mornings, and the curved walls of the turret create an intimate, one-of-a-kind atmosphere you simply won't find anywhere else.

The Cathedral Experience

Moments You'll Treasure

Imagine waking to golden morning light filtering through the curved turret windows, the spires of St. Paul Cathedral silhouetted against the dawn sky. After a restful night in your Queen bed, step onto the heated bathroom floors and prepare for the day ahead.

Descend the grand staircase to enjoy a gourmet breakfast in our elegant dining room, where fresh, locally-sourced ingredients are transformed into culinary delights. Then explore Cathedral Hill's charming shops, cafes, and historic architecture—all within walking distance.

Return in the evening for a soak in your bubble massage tub, the cathedral lights twinkling through your windows as you drift into relaxation.
