Library Suite

Fireside Romance in Victorian Elegance

Second Floor Suite with Gas Fireplace

Your Cozy Victorian Retreat

The Library Suite is the quintessential cozy retreat—a space designed for those who appreciate the simple pleasures of a good book by the fire, a leisurely soak in a deep tub, and the comfort of a well-appointed room. Named for its warm, intimate atmosphere reminiscent of a private library, this suite invites you to slow down and savor every moment.

The crown jewel of the Library Suite is its fully functional gas fireplace—one of the only working fireplaces available to guests. With the flip of a switch, dancing flames create the perfect ambiance for a romantic evening or a peaceful night of relaxation. The warmth of the fire pairs perfectly with Minnesota's cooler seasons.

Like the Storybook Suite, the Library Suite features a balcony sleeping porch, adding versatility and charm to your stay. The bathroom offers a deep soaking tub for long, relaxing baths and a refreshing rain shower—everything you need to feel completely restored.

The Heart of the Room

A Real Working Fireplace

There's something primal and deeply comforting about gathering around a fire. In the Library Suite, that experience is just a switch away. Our fully functional gas fireplace provides instant warmth and ambiance without the fuss of traditional wood fires.

Picture yourself on a crisp autumn evening or a snowy winter night, flames dancing in the grate, the room bathed in warm golden light. Curl up with your partner, sip a glass of wine, and let the stresses of everyday life melt away.

The fireplace is especially popular during Minnesota's colder months, making the Library Suite our most requested accommodation from October through March. Book early to secure your fireside retreat.

The Library Experience

Quiet Moments, Lasting Memories

The Library Suite invites a slower pace. Leave the rush of daily life at the door and settle into a space designed for connection and relaxation. Light the fire, draw a bath, and let time expand.

In the morning, step onto your balcony sleeping porch with a cup of coffee and watch Cathedral Hill come to life. After a gourmet breakfast, venture out to explore Summit Avenue's grand historic homes or browse the boutiques and cafes nearby.

As evening approaches, return to your private sanctuary. Perhaps a glass of wine by the fire, a movie on the Smart TV, or simply quiet conversation with someone special. The Library Suite creates the perfect backdrop for whatever your heart desires.
