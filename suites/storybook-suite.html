The Storybook Suite lives up to its name—a magical retreat that feels lifted from the pages of a fairy tale. For guests who need to stay connected, a dedicated workspace provides the perfect spot for catching up on emails or planning the day's adventures. The private bathroom ensures your comfort with modern amenities thoughtfully integrated into the Victorian aesthetic.

A Minnesota Tradition

The Sleeping Porch Experience

Sleeping porches have been a beloved Minnesota tradition since the early 1900s, when physicians prescribed fresh air for good health. Our enclosed balcony sleeping porch continues this tradition, offering a unique way to experience the changing seasons.

In summer, fall asleep to cool evening breezes and the sounds of Cathedral Hill. In winter, snuggle under warm blankets as snow falls softly outside. Year-round, wake to breathtaking views of the St. Paul Cathedral—an experience found nowhere else in the city.

The sleeping porch features comfortable furnishings and can serve as an alternative sleeping space or a peaceful retreat for reading, meditation, or simply soaking in the views.

The Storybook Experience

Your Own Fairy Tale

There's something magical about waking in the Storybook Suite. As dawn breaks over Cathedral Hill, golden light streams through the windows, painting the room in warm hues. From your sleeping porch, watch as the cathedral's spires catch the first rays of sun—a private showing of nature's daily masterpiece.

After a leisurely morning, join fellow guests for a gourmet breakfast in our elegant dining room. Then venture out to explore—perhaps a walk through nearby Summit Avenue's historic mansions, or coffee at one of Cathedral Hill's charming cafes.

Return as evening falls to find your room bathed in the soft glow of sunset. Retire to the sleeping porch with a good book, or settle into the King bed and let the peaceful sounds of the neighborhood lull you to sleep.
