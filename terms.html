Terms of Service

Last Updated: December 8, 2024

Welcome to The New Victorian Mansion Bed & Breakfast. By making a reservation or staying at our property, you agree to the following terms and conditions. Please read them carefully.

Reservation Policies

Making a Reservation

Reservations can be made through our website, by phone at (651) 286-9333, or by email at info@newvictorianbb.com. All reservations require a valid credit card to guarantee the booking.

Deposit Requirements

A deposit equal to the first night's stay is charged at the time of booking to confirm your reservation. The remaining balance is due upon check-in.

Special Event Bookings: Weddings and private events require a 50% non-refundable deposit at booking, with the remaining balance due 30 days before the event date.

Cancellation Policy

  • 7+ days before arrival: Full refund of deposit
  • 3-6 days before arrival: 50% of deposit refunded
  • Less than 3 days / No-show: No refund; full reservation charged

We strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance to protect against unforeseen circumstances.

Holiday & Peak Season Policy

Reservations during holidays (Thanksgiving, Christmas, New Year's, Valentine's Day) and peak season (May-October) require a minimum 2-night stay and have a 14-day cancellation policy for full refund.

Check-In & Check-Out

  • Check-in: 4:00 PM - 8:00 PM
  • Check-out: 11:00 AM

Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request and subject to availability. Late check-in (after 8:00 PM) must be arranged in advance.

Guest Policies

Age Requirement

The primary guest making the reservation must be at least 21 years of age. Children are welcome in the Penthouse suite; please inquire about child policies for individual suites.

Maximum Occupancy

Each suite has a maximum occupancy as listed on our website. The Penthouse accommodates up to 8 guests. Additional guests beyond the stated occupancy are not permitted.

No Smoking Policy

The New Victorian Mansion is a completely smoke-free property. Smoking is prohibited inside the mansion and on all porches and balconies. A $500 cleaning fee will be charged for violations.

Pet Policy

Due to the historic nature of our property and to accommodate guests with allergies, pets are not permitted. Service animals are welcome with advance notice.

Quiet Hours

Out of respect for all guests, quiet hours are observed from 10:00 PM to 8:00 AM.

Property Care

Our mansion features original architectural details, antique furnishings, and historic fixtures dating back to 1894. Guests are expected to treat the property with care and respect.

  • Guests are responsible for any damage to the property, furnishings, or fixtures during their stay
  • Please report any damage or maintenance issues immediately
  • Do not move furniture or remove items from rooms
  • Food and beverages are not permitted in bedrooms (except breakfast-in-bed service)

Breakfast & Dining

A complimentary gourmet breakfast is served daily at 9:00 AM in our dining room. Please inform us of any dietary restrictions or allergies at the time of booking. Breakfast-in-bed service is available for an additional fee.

Parking

Complimentary on-site parking is available for guests on a first-come, first-served basis. Street parking is also available in the neighborhood.

Liability

The New Victorian Mansion Bed & Breakfast is not responsible for:

  • Loss, theft, or damage to guests' personal belongings
  • Personal injury occurring on the property, except where caused by our negligence
  • Interruptions to services due to circumstances beyond our control

We recommend guests secure valuables and maintain appropriate personal insurance coverage.

Gift Certificates

  • Gift certificates are valid for 2 years from the date of purchase
  • Gift certificates are non-refundable and cannot be exchanged for cash
  • Lost or stolen gift certificates cannot be replaced
  • Gift certificates must be presented at check-in

Website Use

The content on our website is for informational purposes only. We reserve the right to modify room descriptions, rates, amenities, and policies at any time. Photos are representative of our accommodations but individual rooms may vary.

Governing Law

These terms shall be governed by the laws of the State of Minnesota. Any disputes arising from your stay or use of our services shall be resolved in the courts of Ramsey County, Minnesota.
