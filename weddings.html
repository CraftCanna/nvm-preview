Weddings & Events

Create unforgettable memories in our historic Victorian mansion

Imagine exchanging vows beneath a glittering chandelier in a grand Victorian parlor, surrounded only by your closest loved ones. At The New Victorian Mansion, we offer something truly rare: an exclusive private wedding experience where the entire historic mansion becomes yours alone.

No other guests. No competing events. Just you, your beloved, and the timeless elegance of an 1890 architectural masterpiece that has witnessed over 130 years of Saint Paul history.

Exclusively Yours

When you reserve The New Victorian Mansion for your wedding or special event, you receive exclusive access to our entire property. This means complete privacy for your celebration and the freedom to use every stunning space our historic mansion has to offer.

Our four luxury suites accommodate up to eight guests from your wedding party, allowing your closest friends and family to stay on-site and continue the celebration from evening through a leisurely gourmet breakfast the next morning.

From intimate elopements to elegant wedding ceremonies with up to 30 guests, our mansion provides the perfect backdrop for couples seeking a unique, romantic, and deeply personal wedding experience.

Your Canvas

Stunning Venue Spaces

Each room offers its own character and charm for your celebration

Grand Foyer & Staircase

Make a dramatic entrance down our iconic Victorian staircase. The grand foyer with its original woodwork and soaring ceilings creates an unforgettable first impression for your ceremony or reception.

Victorian Parlor

Our formal parlor features period furnishings, ornate fireplaces, and stunning architectural details. Perfect for intimate ceremonies, cocktail hours, or elegant portrait sessions.

Formal Dining Room

Host an intimate reception dinner in our elegant dining room, where your guests can enjoy catered cuisine surrounded by Victorian grandeur and soft candlelight.

Victorian Porch

Our wraparound front porch offers a charming outdoor space for ceremonies, cocktails, or simply enjoying the Cathedral Hill neighborhood on a beautiful Minnesota day.

Garden & Grounds

Our private backyard garden provides a serene outdoor setting for ceremonies, photos, or alfresco celebrations. Beautiful landscaping and mature trees create a natural backdrop.

Royal Penthouse Suite

Our most luxurious accommodation serves as the perfect bridal suite. The tower room, private sitting area, and spa-like bathroom provide the ideal space for pre-ceremony preparations.

A Perfect Location

Situated in Saint Paul's prestigious Cathedral Hill neighborhood, The New Victorian Mansion offers unparalleled convenience for your guests. Just steps from the magnificent Cathedral of Saint Paul, your wedding photos can include one of Minnesota's most stunning architectural landmarks.

The historic Summit Avenue neighborhood, with its grand Victorian homes and tree-lined streets, provides additional scenic backdrops. Downtown Saint Paul is minutes away, with excellent restaurants for post-ceremony celebrations and convenient hotel options for guests not staying at the mansion.
