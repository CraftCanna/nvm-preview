Booking Information

Reservations & Policies

Check-in is at 4:00 PM and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability. Please contact us in advance to arrange.

Cancellations made 7 or more days before arrival receive a full refund. Cancellations within 7 days of arrival will be charged for the first night. No-shows are charged for the full reservation.

You can book directly through our website using the "Book Now" button, which takes you to our secure reservation system. You can also call us at (651) 286-9333 or email info@newvictorianbb.com. We recommend booking directly with us for the best rates and flexibility.

We accept all major credit cards including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover. We also accept payment through our secure online booking system. A valid credit card is required to hold your reservation.

There is no minimum stay requirement for most dates. However, during peak seasons and special events (such as holidays, the Minnesota State Fair, or major local events), a two-night minimum may apply. Please check our booking system for specific date requirements.

Yes! Gift certificates make wonderful presents for anniversaries, birthdays, holidays, or any special occasion. We offer certificates in various denominations ($100, $250, $500, or custom amounts). Visit our Gift Certificates page or contact us to purchase.

For All Guests

Accessibility

Our Farrington Suite on the main level is ADA-accessible with a walk-in shower and no steps. The main floor common areas, including the dining room where breakfast is served, are also accessible. Please contact us directly to discuss specific accessibility needs and we'll do our best to accommodate you.

Due to the historic nature of our property and to accommodate guests with allergies, we are unable to allow pets. Service animals are always welcome with advance notice. We can recommend nearby pet-friendly accommodations if needed.

Yes, children are welcome at The New Victorian Mansion. However, please note that our property features antique furnishings, a grand staircase, and historic details that require supervision of young children. We recommend our property for families with children age 10 and older who can appreciate the historic nature of our home.

The New Victorian Mansion is a completely smoke-free property, including all guest suites, common areas, and porches. This includes cigarettes, cigars, pipes, vaping devices, and cannabis products. A cleaning fee will be assessed if evidence of smoking is found.

Explore Saint Paul

Local Area

Our Cathedral Hill location puts you steps away from the stunning Cathedral of Saint Paul, charming restaurants along Selby Avenue, and the beautiful Summit Avenue Victorian homes (the longest stretch of Victorian residences in the country). Downtown Saint Paul is just minutes away, offering the Ordway Center, Science Museum, Xcel Energy Center, and more.

Minneapolis is approximately 10 miles west of our location, about a 15-20 minute drive depending on traffic. You can also take the Metro Transit Green Line light rail from nearby Union Depot. Popular Minneapolis destinations include the Walker Art Center, Mall of America, Guthrie Theater, and the Minneapolis Institute of Art.

Absolutely! Cathedral Hill and nearby Grand Avenue offer excellent dining. Local favorites include W.A. Frost for upscale American cuisine, Moscow on the Hill for Russian fare, and Meritage for French bistro. We're happy to provide personalized recommendations and make reservations for you during your stay.

Minneapolis-Saint Paul International Airport (MSP) is about 15-20 minutes from the mansion by car. Taxi, Uber, and Lyft services are readily available at the airport. If you prefer public transit, take the Metro Transit Blue Line to downtown Minneapolis, then transfer to the Green Line toward Saint Paul Union Depot.
